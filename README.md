

Electric Vehicle Analysis Dashboard | Power BI Project

An interactive Power BI dashboard that analyzes Electric Vehicle (EV) adoption trends, vehicle distribution, manufacturer performance, and CAFV eligibility across the United States, helping stakeholders understand the evolving EV market.



Short Description or Purpose

This dashboard was created to explore and visualize Electric Vehicle population data in the United States. The goal was to uncover trends in EV adoption, compare Battery Electric Vehicles (BEVs) and Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEVs), identify leading manufacturers, and provide actionable insights through interactive visualizations.



Tech Stack

The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

* Power BI Desktop
* Power Query
* DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)
* Data Modeling
* Data Visualization
* Microsoft Excel / CSV Dataset


Data Source

Dataset: Electric Vehicle Population Data from kaggle 

Source: Open-source EV population dataset containing vehicle registration information, electric vehicle types, model years, manufacturers, electric range, CAFV eligibility status, and geographic location data across the United States.


Business Problem

With the increasing adoption of Electric Vehicles, stakeholders need a clear understanding of market growth, vehicle distribution, manufacturer dominance, and consumer preferences. Raw data alone makes it difficult to identify meaningful trends and make informed decisions.

Goal

The objective of this dashboard is to:

* Monitor overall EV adoption trends.
* Compare BEV and PHEV market share.
* Analyze EV growth over time.
* Identify top-performing manufacturers and vehicle models.
* Understand geographical distribution of EVs.
* Evaluate CAFV eligibility patterns.

 Key Visuals
 KPI Cards

Used to display:

* Total Vehicles
* Average Electric Range
* Total BEV Vehicles
* Total PHEV Vehicles
* BEV Percentage
* PHEV Percentage

Reason: Provides a quick summary of the most important business metrics.

Line Chart – Total Vehicles by Model Year

Shows EV adoption trends over time.

Reason: Ideal for visualizing growth patterns and year-over-year changes.

  Map Visualization – Total Vehicles by State

Displays geographical distribution of EV registrations.

Reason: Helps identify regions with higher EV adoption rates.

  Bar Chart – Top Vehicle Manufacturers

Highlights manufacturers with the highest number of registered EVs.

Reason: Makes brand comparisons easy and visually clear.

 Donut Chart – CAFV Eligibility

Shows the proportion of CAFV-eligible vehicles.

Reason: Effectively represents percentage-based distributions.

Treemap – Top Vehicle Models

Displays the most popular EV models based on registrations.

Reason: Enables quick identification of dominant vehicle models.

 Interactive Slicers

Filters:

* City
* Electric Utility
* Electric Vehicle Type

Reason: Allows users to perform dynamic analysis and drill into specific segments.


   Business Impact and Insights

* The EV market is dominated by Battery Electric Vehicles (BEVs), accounting for approximately 78% of total registrations.
* Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEVs) contribute around 22% of the EV population.
* EV adoption has increased significantly over recent years, indicating strong market growth.
* Tesla emerged as the leading manufacturer with the highest number of registered electric vehicles.
* Certain states demonstrate substantially higher EV adoption rates than others.
* CAFV eligibility data provides insights into the influence of incentive programs on EV adoption.
* Popular vehicle models indicate evolving consumer preferences in the electric mobility sector.
